### Finder
- ⌘⇧. - Show hidden items
- ⌘⇧G - Show "Go To" dialog
- Space - preview

### All apps
- ⌥ + Minimize - Minimize all app windows
- ⌘⇧/ - Search help
- ⌘, - Open settings

- ⌘ + Click - Show item in Finder (Works in dock or anywhere else)
- ⌘ + ↩︎ - Show item in Finder (works in Spotlight)

### Dialogs
- ↩︎  - Press selected button
- Space - Press the other button
